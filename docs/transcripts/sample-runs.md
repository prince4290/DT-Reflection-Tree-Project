Sample Transcripts: The Reflection Journey
Below are two sample "walkthroughs" of the Reflection Tree. These transcripts demonstrate how the deterministic logic handles different employee mindsets and produces tailored summaries.

Persona 1: The "Victor" (High Agency, High Contribution)
This user is having a good day and recognizes their own role in their success.

[SYSTEM]: Good evening. Let's process the day so you can rest with a clear mind.

[QUESTION]: Looking back, how would you label the 'vibe' of your workday?

> USER: 1. Productive & Fluid

[SYSTEM]: You felt in flow. What was the main reason you stayed on track?

> USER: 2. I adapted when things shifted

[SYSTEM]: Owning your productivity is a skill. It means you recognize your agency in the process.

[SYSTEM]: Now, let’s shift from how you handled tasks to how you handled people.

[QUESTION]: Which of these best describes your interaction with the team today?

> USER: 2. I shared knowledge openly

[SYSTEM]: That sounds like a 'Citizenship' moment. What motivated you to step up?

> USER: 2. I saw a gap and filled it

[SYSTEM]: Finally, let's look at the 'Why' behind your work.

[QUESTION]: Who benefited the most from your hard work today?

> USER: 1. The end-customer

[SUMMARY]: Today, you described the day as 'Productive & Fluid'. You leaned toward internal agency and a altrocentric radius of concern. Remember: 'The end-customer' benefiting is a win to take into tomorrow.

[SYSTEM]: Reflection complete. Go rest.

Persona 2: The "Victim" (External Locus, Self-Centric)

> USER: 3. Chaotic & Out of Control
